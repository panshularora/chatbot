## How to Use

1. **Open your browser** (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
2. **Navigate to** `http://localhost:5000`
3. **Start chatting!** Try messages like:
   - "I am so happy today!"
   - "This makes me sad"
   - "I am furious about this"
   - "I feel scared"

## How to Restart the Server

If you need to restart the server:

1. **Stop the server**: Press `Ctrl+C` in the terminal running the app
2. **Start again**: 
   ```powershell
   cd C:\Users\Panshul\Desktop\newos\emotion-chatbot
   python app.py
   ```

## What Happens When You Chat

- Your message is analyzed for emotion (happy, sad, angry, fear, surprise, love, or neutral)
- The chatbot responds empathetically based on the detected emotion
- Both your message and the detected emotion are displayed
